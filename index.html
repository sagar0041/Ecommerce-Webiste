    Vespa Notte 125

    Vespa Notte 125 price starts at Rs.68,<845>. Paired with a CVT unit, the scooter draws power from a 125cc three-valve single-cylinder engine that generates 10.6PS at 7500rpm and 10.6Nm at 6000rpm.
